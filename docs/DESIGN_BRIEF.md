# Scriptured Design Brief

## Product Feeling

Scriptured should feel like a colourful Bible reading habit app, not a traditional Bible study tool.

The app should feel:
- motivating
- playful
- warm
- energetic
- habit-forming
- spiritually respectful

## Inspiration

Inspired by:
- Duolingo-style habit streaks and reward feedback
- Apple Fitness-style progress visibility
- Finch-style friendly self-improvement UI
- modern SwiftUI card-based dashboards

Do not directly copy any app.

## Visual Style

Use:
- rounded cards
- soft gradients
- large friendly headings
- clear reward feedback
- XP bars
- coin pills
- streak flames
- bright but not overwhelming colors
- Green and Beige colours and the main theme.

## Mood

The app should encourage the user with urgency, but not guilt or shame.

Good copy:
- "Protect your streak"
- "Your streak is at risk"
- "One chapter keeps the fire alive"
- "You are done for today"
- "Come back tomorrow to keep growing"

Avoid:
- shame-heavy language
- overly childish design
- casino-like reward visuals
